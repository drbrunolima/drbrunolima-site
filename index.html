Dr. Bruno Menezes, MD

Endocrinologist & Clinical Researcher • Aspiring Medical Science Liaison

Professional Overview

Dr. Bruno Menezes is a board‑certified physician with more than a decade of clinical experience in endocrinology and nutrition. As founder and medical director of Spazio Essenza, he has overseen the care of thousands of patients while leading a multidisciplinary team and cultivating partnerships with key opinion leaders. Dr. Menezes holds postgraduate training in Nutrology (ABRAN) and Endocrinology (IPEMED) and is certified in Good Clinical Practice and Responsible Conduct of Research. He has authored peer‑reviewed studies in diverse therapeutic areas and frequently engages the media to disseminate evidence‑based recommendations on nutrition and metabolic health.

Currently Dr. Menezes is pursuing a transition from clinical practice into Medical Affairs. With a proven record of scientific communication, clinical research coordination and strategic networking, he aims to relocate to the United States and contribute to pharmaceutical innovation as a Medical Science Liaison. This site highlights his academic qualifications, professional achievements, publications, media interviews and future ambitions.

Academic & Professional Qualifications

  • Medical Degree: Universidade do Grande Rio (UNIGRANRIO)
  • Postgraduate Diplomas: Nutrology – Brazilian Association of Nutrology (ABRAN); Endocrinology & Metabology – IPEMED Medical School
  • Certifications: Good Clinical Practice (CITI Program), Responsible Conduct of Research (CITI Program), Clinical Research Coordinator Foundations
  • Leadership: Founder & Medical Director of Spazio Essenza; Director of the Vegan/Vegetarian Nutrition Department at the Brazilian Society of Obesity Medicine
  • Research & Publications: Author of peer‑reviewed articles on polypharmacy, quality of life in melasma, adnexal tumor diagnostics, mental health after disasters and autism screening (see below)
  • Clinical & Business Impact: Managed a practice that delivered more than 5 000 consultations in metabolic and nutritional disorders, while developing patient education programs and clinical protocols
  • Soft Skills: Scientific communication, relationship building with key opinion leaders, leadership and team management, strategic planning
  • Professional Ambition: Relocation to the USA to join the pharmaceutical industry as a Medical Science Liaison/Medical Affairs specialist, leveraging clinical expertise and research acumen to support evidence‑based therapies
